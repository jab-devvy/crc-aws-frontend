About Me

Hi there! My name is Alex. I've had a fascination with computers and IT as a whole since I was first able to use a Logitech trackball on my Dad's Power Macintosh G3. - I really became interested in IT as a career after reading Little Brother by Cory Doctorow in middle school. - It introduced me to Linux and the idea of hacker culture, and I promptly worked for months trying to defeat my school's locked down boot menu so that I could load an Ubuntu live CD and play games or something. - I unfortunately was never able to accomplish this, or install the same CD onto my Mom's old iBook (I had yet to understand CPU architecture). Following this, - I took what were ultimately four very formative IT classes in high school (thanks Mr. Tokar!), and I've been hooked ever since. I have yet to have held an 'official' help desk or system admin position, - but have somehow managed to apply my knowledge in pretty much every job I've had in some way.
- My other interests/hobbies include music, animation, and looking at cool creatures I come across on walks.
